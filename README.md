# Notemeleon - A notes taking app

Notemeleon is a cool notes taking app that keeps your data safe in Firebase. The best part? It can summarize your notes, so you don't have to read through long texts. It's perfect for students, professionals, or anyone who wants to take notes easily and stay organized. Check it out and simplify your note-taking game!
